🆓 Make This Website Live — FREE, Step by Step
🥇 Method 1: GitHub Pages (Best — Free Forever)
📥 Step 1: Right click on this page → "Save As" → Save as index.html on your computer
🌐 Step 2: Open github.com → Click Sign Up → Create free account (use any email)
📁 Step 3: Click green "New" button → Repository name: my-post-business → Click "Create repository"
⬆️ Step 4: Click "uploading an existing file" → Drag and drop your index.html → Click green "Commit changes"
⚙️ Step 5: Click Settings tab (top of repo) → Click Pages (left menu) → Under "Source" select main → Click Save
🎉 Step 6: Wait 2 minutes → Your site is LIVE at: https://YOUR-NAME.github.io/my-post-business
✅ This is completely free. No credit card. No expiry. Works on phone and computer.
🥈 Method 2: Netlify Drop (Easiest — 1 Minute)
📥 Step 1: Save this file as index.html
🌐 Step 2: Open netlify.com/drop
📁 Step 3: Drag and drop your index.html file into the big box on the page
🎉 Step 4: Done! Your site goes live in seconds at a free URL like abc123.netlify.app
✅ No account needed for basic use. Free custom URL available with free account.
🥉 Method 3: Use Directly on Phone (No Internet Needed)
📲 Just save the index.html file on your phone
📂 Open it with Chrome browser → tap the file → it opens as a website!
✅ Works 100% offline. All data saved on your phone automatically.
